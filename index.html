Welcome to the official website for BibSearch application!

This application is the culmination of a year's worth of work by the 2020 senior class of the Information Resource Management stream of Carleton University's Bachelor of Information Technology. With our application, no longer do you need to ask librarians for help to locate a book's location on the floor! Simply find the book in the application's search function (exclusively MacOdrum library compatible!) and it can narrow down the location of the material to both the row and bay number!
